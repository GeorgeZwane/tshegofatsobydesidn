Moscow Fashion Week

2024 Moscow Fashion Week (Moscow):

In this exclusive feature, we go backstage with the visionary designer Tshegofatso Malefane during his much-anticipated homecoming showcase at the Sta-Sof-Fro Soweto Fashion Week. Returning to his roots, Tshegofatso brought the vibrancy of Daveyton to the runway, captivating audiences with his bold designs and innovative storytelling. His collection celebrated cultural pride and the intersection of heritage and high fashion, reflecting themes of resilience, transformation, and unapologetic self-expression. The atmosphere buzzed with energy as he reconnected with his local audience, cementing his role as a trailblazer bridging the gap between South African style and the global stage.
