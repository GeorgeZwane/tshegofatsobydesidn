who am i ?

TSHEGOFATSO MALEFANE

Tshegofatso Malefane, an internationally acclaimed designer from the dynamic streets of Daveyton, has solidified his position as a luminary in the global fashion industry. Renowned for his innovative vision and avant-garde approach, Malefane is widely celebrated as a “high fashion genius” whose work consistently redefines contemporary aesthetics. Most recently, he captivated the industry’s elite at Moscow Fashion Week with *Frankenstein*, his latest collection. This masterful exploration of transformation and evolution weaves intricate butterfly motifs with themes of resilience, rebirth, and the liberating essence of metamorphosis, affirming his status as a pioneer in high fashion.
